Baked French Toast





A savory treat for any time of day



I got this baked French toast recipe from my sister in law. She and my brother had it at their gift opening after the wedding. I make it any time we have people staying with us, and it is always a hit. You may sprinkle the top with pecans if you wish.

Ingredients



  • French bread: This recipe starts a loaf of French bread, cut into diagonal pieces.
  • Eggs: The base of the French toast custard is eight large eggs.
  • Milk and half-and-half: Youâ€™ll need milk and half-and-half to complete the custard.
  • Vanilla and cinnamon: Take the warm and cozy flavor up a notch with vanilla extract and ground cinnamon.
  • Butter: Butter lends richness and flavor.
  • Brown sugar and corn syrup: Sweeten things up with brown sugar and light corn syrup.


Directions



  1. Arrange the bread slices in a buttered baking dish.
  2. Beat the eggs, milk, cream, vanilla, and cinnamon together and pour over the bread.
  3. Refrigerate overnight.
  4. Combine the remaining ingredients in a saucepan and heat until bubbling.
  5. Pour the brown sugar mixture over the bread.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven.


Enjoy!
